Information about my personal Interests:
Cattle breeding

Cattle breeding became a hobby for me a few years ago. I started by breeding a small number of chickens and rabbits, and then moved on to more serious projects such as keeping cows and goats. I like to study different breeds and their characteristics, as well as take care of animals and monitor their health. Participation in local fairs and exhibitions provides an opportunity to share experiences with other pastoralists and share the results of their work. This hobby brings me a lot of joy and satisfaction!

Boxing

Boxing became a real hobby for me a few years ago. I started doing this sport to keep fit and develop discipline. Participating in training and sparring helped me improve my technique and endurance. I also participate in local tournaments, which adds excitement and motivation to training. Boxing not only strengthens the body, but also develops self-confidence, and I get great pleasure from every activity!

Auto tuning

Auto tuning became a hobby for me when I first thought about how to improve the appearance and characteristics of my car. I started with small changes such as installing sports seats and updating the audio system. Gradually, I delved into the technical aspects, exploring the possibilities of tuning the engine, suspension and braking system. I like to experiment with different styles and functional modifications, as well as participate in local car shows and exhibitions where I can share my experience with other enthusiasts. Auto tuning gives me the opportunity to express my personality and improve the performance of the car, and I get great pleasure from the process!

Football

Football has become an important part of my life from an early age. I started playing on the school team and later joined the local league. I like not only the process of the game itself, but also the team spirit that we create on the field. I train regularly and participate in matches with friends and other teams. Football helps me keep fit and develops strategic thinking, as well as gives me the opportunity to meet new people and enjoy communicating with like-minded people!

Soldering

Soldering became a hobby for me when I started repairing electronics and creating my own projects. I got carried away with disassembling old devices, studying how they work, and soon realized that I could assemble various electronic circuits myself. I enjoy working with various components such as resistors, capacitors and microcontrollers, creating unique devices, from simple LED designs to more complex systems. This process requires patience and attention to detail, but it is extremely satisfying when I see my circuits come to life. I also often attend local workshops and communities where I can share experiences with other enthusiasts. Soldering not only develops my technical skills, but also allows me to implement creative ideas, which makes this hobby really exciting!
